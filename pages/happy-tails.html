Successful Rescues

These are some stories of our some great rescues by people just like you! There were so many to choose from, but these were our favourites.

Buiscuit

Buiscuit was found on the streets of Toronto and had many skin infections and was losing his fur. He was brought in and taken care of right away, with antibiotics, water and lots of love. 4 months later he was doing so much better and he was adopted into a loving family of 4.

Puggle

Puggle was abandoned by his mother after just one week. When he was brought in he was very weak. With some love and care he was ready to be adopted 4 weeks later by a couple living in a condo. They frequently send pictures of him running in the park.

Spot

Spot was the runt of a huge litter of 11! He was always last to food and had a limp for the first year of his life. He was adopted at 1 and a half by a dad and two children that adore him.

Snowy

Snowy is a big sweety and it was hard for the shelter to give him up! He had become a favourite by all but he fell in love with his new owner. The two of them are now the best of pals, living together along the Ottawa River.
